Department of Physical Education and Sports

Our institute's aim is overall development of the students and physical education, games and sports is playing a vital role in it and also, physical education is integral part education to get real life experiences. Games and Sports are playing a significant role to achieve optimum physical development of the students. There is no doubt that physical education & sports make more effective contribution to the inculcation of fundamental human value as underlying the full development of people.
Indore Institute of Science Technology is providing in-house sports facilities for all the students and staff so that they get opportunity to develop the abilities, will power and self-discipline. Institute has many sporting facilities for students and staff such as a well-equipped Gym, Strength & Conditioning apparatus, Basketball court, Lawn Tennis courts, Lush green Football field, Cricket Ground, Table-Tennis arena, Badminton court, Chess and Carrom board arena and Yoga hall. The Department of games and Sports is organizing Yoga classes, Zumba and Aerobics sessions for students. Department has various indoor recreational activities for specially blessed students and an under-constructed sports complex.
The Department of Games & Sports encourages students to participate in various Inter departmental, Intercollegiate and Intervarsity tournaments and likewise open State, National and International competitions. Our many students have participated and won the medals at various stages such as in Intercollegiate, Intervarsity, State, National and International competitions.
The Department of Games & Sports has successfully organized various intercollegiate competitions and open tournaments for college students as well as an Invitational Inter School Apratim Pragya Sports Competitions for school students.
The Department Games & Sports always contribute to the maintenance and improvement of health, provide a wholesome leisure time occupation as well as enable students to overcome the drawbacks of modern living.
