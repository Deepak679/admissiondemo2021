FINANCIAL AID AND SCHOLARSHIPS TO WARDS OF ARMED FORCES

The Board of Governors and The Director General of IIST Group of Institutions (IIST IIP IIMR) are honoured to announce institution of merit based Financial Aid and Scholarships for wards of serving and ESM of the Armed Forces on first come, first served basis. Both Boys and Girls can apply.

"

1. Need Based aid for Hostel accommodation is provisioned for deserving students (Both girls and boys). We train graduates for SSB interviews for Technical Entry Scheme/CDS. A plus point is that IIST group of Institutions is close to MHOW and services of MH, ESM cell can be availed.

2. Other achievements in sports/co-curricular activities also will be considered for scholarship.
